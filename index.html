Yaroslav Gnedov is a producer and visual designer based in Moscow. He collaborates with international artists and music labels, creating immersive audiovisual experiences. His work also includes photography and video editing.

I started producing music at the age of sixteen, and since then, sound and visuals have always been connected parts of my process. I've always been interested in how images and video can expand the emotional dimension of my music.

Influenced by music bands, architects, photographers, and places around the world, I have developed a personal vision that I translate into my work.
